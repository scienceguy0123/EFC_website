WE ARE

Enjoy Filming Club is a student run organization based in Boston University. It is composed of a group of diverse, passionate, highly collaborative creatives. Our vision is to create an oasis for passionate movie creators and movie lovers. One of the biggest challenge of making films is lack of resources. Talents, equipments, settings, etc, are essential for producing good films, but they are difficult to obtain, especially for students. EFC recognizes student filmmakersâ€™ needs and provides resources for its members to help them to land their projects on the big screen.

For now, EFC has produced over 20 films, and its efforts is recognized by the community and the professionals. Beyond that, We also host weekly screening, which is a great chance for members to meetup and have fun, or for prospective members to take glimpse of our club. EFC is a big family and new members are always welcomed. For more information, please contact us.
